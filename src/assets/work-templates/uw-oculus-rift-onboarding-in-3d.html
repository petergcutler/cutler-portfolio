On-boarding with the Oculus Platform
Conducting a usability test to evaluate first time user experiences with Oculus Touch and motion-controlled software
  • UX Research
  • Research Planning
  • Usability Testing
  • Qualitative Research
  • Quantitative Research

In January 2018, I formed a group with three other graduate students to develop and conduct a usability test. We were interested in testing a product with a complex, novel interface — something that presented different questions and opportunities than traditional web applications. We decided to work with VR, specifically, the Oculus platform.

We were interested in understanding how first-time users would grapple with this new interface — what hurdles they would identify during on-boarding, and how they would adapt and operate the system using a foreign software interface.

At the outset of the project, we decided to focus on two applications on the Oculus platform:

  • First Contact: the Oculus Touch on-boarding application
