Onbording with the Oculus Platform
Conducting a usability test to evaluate first time user experiences with the Oculus Touch motion-controllers
  • UX Research
  • Research Planning
  • Usability Testing
  • Qualitative Research
  • Quantitative Research

In January 2018, I began a 3-month course in Usability Testing, and formed a group with three others to explore user experience in VR. We were interestd in exploring the Oculus Touch, the motion-controllers that accompany the Oculus Rift. We were interested in understanding how users would engage with software using a novel interface for the first time.

We decided to explore the onboarding experience through the use of two applications: First Contact - the platforms primary onboarding experience, and Tilt Brush - a 3d painting application.
