Revamping Date & Time Selections

Developing a modular way to manipulate date & datetime selections in Crowdskout
  • Front-End Engineering
  • JavaScript
  • AngularJS
  • Moment.js

As we grew the Crowdskout app to support more complex time-series graphs and other date & time dependent inputs, we encountered a need to overhaul our date & time UI and data treatment. I led the effort to overhaul our date & time management.

ISO8601 and Moment.js

Using the Moment.js library as a backbone for transforming and handling our date & datetime data, I built a custom component for accepting input data, transforming, and saving data in ISO8601 format. The component was built to be a flexible AngularJS directive that could easily shift implementations between date alone, or datetime, depending on varying needs.

Crowdskout's date & time component is a complex, interactive module within the application. If you have any questions, I'm happy to walk through the design and code involved. Thanks for reading.
