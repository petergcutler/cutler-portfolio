From 2014-2015, I worked with AT&T to redesign their CSR brand and reconsider the way they use blogging and social media to share their stories.

Stakeholder Interviews

The initiative emerged in collaboration with our client stakeholders at AT&T. They were internally reviewing their CSR brand and direction. Over the past four years, we had helped them become one of the most revered CSR brands in the Fortune 100. They wanted to capitalize on that success, and we collaborated with them to revamp their CSR identity.
